Apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate

You can use this service to apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate online.

It will guide you through the information you need to provide and the documents you need to upload.

It takes approximately 30 minutes to complete.

Before you apply

You should:

  • have the correct documents
  • have collected evidence that you’ve been living in your acquired gender
  • have your birth or adoption certificate to refer to
  • be able to pay the application fee

Next steps

After you apply, the documents you’ve uploaded and supplied will be reviewed by the Gender Recognition Panel.

You will then be told if you will be issued with a Gender Recognition Certificate, and what you can do if your application has not be accepted.

Decisions take around 12 weeks on average.
