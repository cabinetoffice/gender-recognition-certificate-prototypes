Overview

Apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate if you want your affirmed gender to be legally recognised in the UK.

Making an application

You will need to provide us with documents that show you have been living in your affirmed gender before applying for a GRC. You can do this online.

Once you have gone through the application process and submitted your documents, the Gender Recognition Panel will review your application.

You will then be told if it has been successful, and what you can do if it has not.
