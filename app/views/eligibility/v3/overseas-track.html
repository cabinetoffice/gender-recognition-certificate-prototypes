You can apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate

You can apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate using this service.

The documents you need to provide are:

  • your original birth certificate
  • your amended birth certificate that shows a change in gender
  • a court order authorising the change of gender
  • a document that’s equivalent to a Gender Recognition Certificate in the country where you’ve received legal recognition of your affirmed gender
  • an entry in a legal register that proves your acquired gender has been recognised in that country
