Name change documents

You’ll need to provide documents to help match your birth records if you have ever changed your name from what appears on your birth certificate.

These might include:

  • deed poll document, either enrolled or unenrolled
  • change of name document (Scotland)
  • marriage certificate
  • civil partnership certificate
