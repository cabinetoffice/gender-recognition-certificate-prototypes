Name changes

If you have ever changed your name from what appears on your birth certificate, you’ll be asked to upload documents for each time you have changed your name.

This is to help match your birth records after your application is accepted.

These might include:

  • deed poll document, either enrolled or unenrolled
  • change of name document (Scotland)
  • marriage certificate
  • civil partnership certificate
