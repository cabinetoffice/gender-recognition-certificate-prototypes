Living in your gender

This is where you can upload evidence that you’ve lived in your affirmed gender for the 2 years before your application.

You should:

  • upload a maximum of 8 documents – try to provide 4 from each year
  • make sure all documents are dated and include your name
  • try to include documents that are dated throughout the year

Examples of what you could provide:

  • driving licence
  • passport
  • payslips, P60 or P45
  • benefits or tax letters and documents
  • bank statements and utility bills
  • letters from official, professional or business organisations like solicitors, accountants, dentists, doctors and employers
  • academic certificates or documentation
  • health care or identity cards including photo ID issued by an official organisation
