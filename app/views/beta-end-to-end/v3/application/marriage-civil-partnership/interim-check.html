Interim Gender Recognition Certificate

{% if data['current-check'] == 'Civil partnership' %}

If you want to remain in your civil partnership but your partner has not signed a statutory declaration of consent, then you cannot receive a full Gender Recognition Certificate without ending your civil partnership first.

An interim Gender Recognition Certificate will help you to do this. After you have used the interim certificate to end your civil partnership you will receive a full certificate.

{% else %}

If you want to remain married but your spouse has not signed a statutory declaration of consent, then you cannot receive a full Gender Recognition Certificate without ending your marriage first.

An interim Gender Recognition Certificate will help you to do this. After you have used the interim certificate to end your marriage you will receive a full certificate.
