Apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate

You can use this service to apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate online.

It will guide you through the information and documents you need to provide by asking questions about your circumstances.

During the application you will be able to:

  • provide your personal and contact details
  • upload evidence that you’ve been living in your acquired gender, if applicable
  • upload your medical reports, if applicable
  • pay the application fee

You will be able to return to your application at any time.

Next steps

After you apply you will receive an email confirmation. You should then send any remaining documents to:

Gender Recognition Panel
PO Box 9300
Leicester
LE1 8DJ

You should include your name and return address alongside these documents.

Once you’ve supplied those documents your application will be reviewed by the Gender Recognition Panel. You will be contacted if the panel requires more information or documentation.

You will then be told if you will be issued with a Gender Recognition Certificate, and what you can do if your application has not been accepted.

Decisions take around 12 weeks on average.
