Who can apply

During the application process we will ask for documents that show you meet the following requirements:

  • you are 18 or over
  • you have been diagnosed with gender dysphoria
  • you have been living in your affirmed gender for at least 2 years
  • you intend to live in this gender for the rest of your life

If you have received gender recognition in another country

If you have had your gender recognised in one of these countries or territories and you want it recognised in the UK, the requirements are different.

The requirements are:

  • you are 18 or over
  • you have a legal document showing your gender recognition from that country

If your gender has been recognised in a country that is not on the list, you will have to meet the full set of requirements at the top of the page.
