Who can apply

You can apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate if you:

  • are 18 or over
  • have been diagnosed with gender dysphoria
  • are living in your affirmed gender and have been for at least 2 years
  • you intend to live in your affirmed gender for the rest of your life

You will be asked to provide documentation that shows you meet these requirements during your application.

You do not need to have had surgery to change your sexual characteristics to apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate, but you will need to tell us why you have not.
