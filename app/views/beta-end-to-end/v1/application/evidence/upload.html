Living in your gender

This is where you can upload evidence that you’ve lived in your affirmed gender for the 2 years before your application.

You should:

  • upload a maximum of 8 documents – try to provide 4 from each year
  • make sure all documents are dated and include your name
  • try to include documents that are dated throughout the year
