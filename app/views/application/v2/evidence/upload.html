Evidence of living in your gender

You will need to provide evidence of living in your affirmed gender from the last 2 years. All documents must be dated and include your name.

You should provide no more than 8 documents from the last 2 years. Try to include documents from dates that are spread throughout those 2 years.

For example, try and provide 4 documents per year and do not provide documents that are all dated in the same month.
