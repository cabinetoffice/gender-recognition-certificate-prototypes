Apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate

You can use this service to apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate online.

It will guide you through the information you need to provide and the documents you need to upload.

Before you apply

During the application you will be able to:

  • provide your application details
  • upload evidence that you’ve been living in your acquired gender
  • pay the application fee

You will be able to return to to your application at any time.

Next steps

After you apply you will receive an application acknowledgment in the post which will include your GRP reference number and will request any further documents required which you will need to post. Once you’ve supplied those documents your application will be reviewed by the Gender Recognition Panel.

You will then be told if you will be issued with a Gender Recognition Certificate, and what you can do if your application has not be accepted.

Decisions take around 12 weeks on average.
