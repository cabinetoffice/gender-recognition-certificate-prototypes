Overview

Apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate if you want your affirmed gender to be legally recognised in the UK.

Although you do not need a Gender Recognition Certificate to get new legal documents like a driving licence or passport, this is not the same as legal recognition.

Having a Gender Recognition Certificate will allow:

  • issue of a new birth certificate which shows your correct gender
  • HMRC to update your gender in its records

Read more about how a Gender Recognition Certificate could impact any pensions and benefits.
