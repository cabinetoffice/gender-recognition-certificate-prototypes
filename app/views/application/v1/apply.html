Apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate

Use this service to start your application and find out what you need to provide with your application.

As part of the application process, you will be asked for both personal and contact details, as well as information on your circumstances, such as whether you are married or in a civil partnership.

It takes approximately 15 minutes to complete.

Next steps

After you finish the questions in the application we will:

  • give you with an application form which includes the information you provide and a checklist of documents that you need to gather
  • ask you to print out the application form and gather your documents
  • request payment for your application
  • give you the address where you need to send your application and documents

It will then be reviewed by the Gender Recognition Panel. They may contact you if they require more information.

You will then be told if you will be issued with a Gender Recognition Certificate.

If your application is unsuccessful, you will be told how you can reapply or appeal the decision.

Decisions take around 12 weeks on average.
