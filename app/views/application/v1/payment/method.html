Method of payment

If paying by cheque or postal order, you must include this with your application form.

Cheques and postal orders payable to ‘HM Courts & Tribunals Service’.

Credit or debit card payments must be made over the telephone within 10 days of receiving your application acknowledgment.

If you would like to pay by credit or debit card then contact the GRP administrator on 020 3936 8963 and have your GRP reference number to hand.
