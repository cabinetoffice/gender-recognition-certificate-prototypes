Interim Gender Recognition Certificate

You cannot receive a full Gender Recognition Certificate if you want to remain married but do not have a declaration of consent from your spouse.

However, you can apply for an interim Gender Recognition Certificate if you are:

  • currently married but do not intend to stay married,
  • are not able to provide a declaration of consent from your spouse

The interim certificate can be used as grounds to end your marriage. You will then be able to receive a full Gender Recognition Certificate.
